Overview

The Division of Administrative Rules provides vital web and publication services to all of the divisions within the Department of State as well as supporting New York's rule making process. DAR produces the weekly State Register that provides information on the rule making activities of state agencies. The division also is the official compiler of state agency rules and regulations (NYCRR) and it publishes information on the Great Seal of New York, the State Constitution, and other official state documents.

New York Codes, Rules, and Regulations

The NYCRR primarily contains state agency rules and regulations adopted under the State Administrative Procedure Act (SAPA). The 23 Titles include one for each state department, one for miscellaneous agencies and one for the Judiciary. The Office of Court Administration and the Judiciary are exempt from SAPA requirements.
